# Nuriek Creative Direction

Nuriek is NOT a generic AI startup website.

The website should feel:
- editorial
- cinematic
- minimalist
- deeply human
- typography-led

Design philosophy:
- black and white only
- premium whitespace
- emotional storytelling
- calm motion
- notebook/archive aesthetic
- founder journey narrative

Avoid:
- gradients
- flashy AI visuals
- neon effects
- generic SaaS layouts
- corporate buzzwords

Typography:
- Playfair Display
- Inter

Color palette:
- background: #f7f7f4
- text: #111111

Motion:
- subtle only
- smooth scrolling
- fade-in transitions
- elegant parallax

The words are more important than graphics.
